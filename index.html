Cybersecurity Resources

  • SANS Institute – Industry-leading training, research, and certifications in cybersecurity, blue teaming, and incident response.
  • CISA Cybersecurity – Alerts, best practices, and tools from the US Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency.
  • Cybrary – Free and paid courses, hands-on labs, and career paths in cybersecurity.
  • Krebs on Security – In-depth news and analysis by journalist Brian Krebs.
  • Dark Reading – Latest cybersecurity news, threat intelligence, and technical insights.
  • SecurityWeek – News, analysis, and opinion on enterprise security.
  • Reddit /r/netsec – Community-driven news and discussions for security professionals.

Digital Forensics Resources

  • Forensic Magazine – News, articles, and case studies on digital forensics and investigations.
  • Digital Forensics Corp – Professional consulting, casework, and educational resources.
  • Coursera: Digital Forensics – University-led online course covering core DFIR concepts.
  • NIST Digital Forensics – Standards, guidelines, and research from NIST.
  • Forensic Focus – Forums, articles, and webinars for DFIR professionals.
  • The Sleuth Kit & Autopsy – Open-source digital forensics tools for file and volume analysis.

Incident Response Resources

  • Incident Response – Best practices, frameworks, and case studies on IR.
  • CERT Incident Management – Guidance and resources from Carnegie Mellon’s CERT/CC.
  • SANS Incident Response – Training, whitepapers, and research on IR and threat hunting.
  • US-CERT ICS Incident Response – Incident response for industrial control systems and OT.
  • MITRE ATT&CK – Knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques for detection and response.
  • FIRST.org – Global incident response teams and coordination.
